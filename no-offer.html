We Can Only Help You Negotiate Once You Have a Loan Estimate

Before we can help you negotiate, you need to have a loan estimate from your lender. A loan estimate is the first set of legally binding terms you receive from them.

We will guide you through contacting other lenders to find a better deal. However, they will usually require proof of your current loan estimate to ensure that you aren’t misrepresenting your offer.

Next Steps with LoanTiger

If You Are Purchasing a Home:

Once the seller accepts your offer, immediately contact your current lender to get a loan estimate. After receiving it, return to LoanTiger as soon as possible. Negotiations are most effective within one day of an accepted offer so you can finalize everything without delaying your closing.

If You Are Refinancing:

You must commit to a lender to obtain a loan estimate. Once you have it, come back to LoanTiger as soon as possible. While refinancing provides a bit more flexibility in timing, it’s still best not to wait too long.
